HMDBS

Harpeth Mens Dining and Benevolence Society

The Harpeth Mens Dining and Benevolence Society (HMDBS) was founded in late summer of 2021 and started meeting once a month for dinner that fall in the new barn at Harpeth Glen Farm.

In addition to dining together once a month, the founders decided to hold an annual fundraiser for the benefit of the youth of South Cheatham County and their organizations in the form of a “$100.00 Breakfast” to be held on July 16, 2022. The goal being to raise $10,000.00 the first year. Tickets will be limited to the first 100 diners.

SKYKING PIZZA at 385 North Main in Kingston Springs, Tennessee, has graciously agreed to cater the first “$100.00 Breakfast.” SKYKING PIZZA is known throughout the area for their excellent breakfast shop, their delicious pizza and their generosity during times of disasters.

Tickets and sponsorships will be available from Society members as the date approaches.
